Our Story

HoneyDew was founded in 2023 by a group of slime enthusiasts who wanted to bring the joy of slime-making to kids everywhere. Our goal is to create safe, fun, and colorful slimes that inspire creativity and fun!

Our Mission

We aim to create high-quality slimes and slime accessories that bring happiness to families worldwide.
