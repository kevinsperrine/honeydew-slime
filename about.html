Who We Are

HoneyDeew is a magical company that specializes in creating high-quality slimes for kids of all ages. Our mission is to bring fun and creativity into the world of slime, teaching kids how to make their own, as well as offering the best slime-themed products!

Our Story

Founded in 2023, HoneyDeew started as a small hobby project and quickly grew into a beloved slime brand. We are passionate about making slime more than just a fun craft — it’s an art form!
